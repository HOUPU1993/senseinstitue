6 Key Findings ✨

100%
100% of Mainland China Users Have Used DeepSeek — All 28 mainland respondents, without exception. U.S. usage rate is only 40%. The two AI ecosystems have clearly diverged.
2×
Paid Users Use It More Intensively — 61% of paid users use LLMs countless times daily, nearly double free users (33%).
4.53
Claude Has the Highest Coding Score — Across all models and all dimensions, Claude's coding score of 4.53 stands alone at the top, yet overall usage rate is only 35%.
4.20
Gemini Leads Image Generation by Far — 4.20 vs. 2.44–2.80 for other models, a gap of over 1.4 points. No contest.
52%
The More You Use, the More You Depend — Among those using countless times daily, 52% report strong dependence; only 11% among those using a few times a week.
$200
Males Have a Higher Payment Ceiling — Some men pay $200–300/month; all female respondents cap at under $40, mostly concentrated in the $0–20 range.
